About Pravah

Pravah is a single-day community event combining athletic contests and technology challenges to encourage teamwork, learning, and fun. Events are suitable for beginners to experienced participants. Safety marshals and first aid will be available.

Date: 10 July 2026
Time: 9:00 AM — 6:00 PM
Age: All ages (some youth categories)

Games — Sports & Tech

Relay Race

Team relay (4 members). Heats in the morning, finals midday. Prizes for top 3 teams.

Throwball / Badminton

Singles and doubles categories for badminton; mixed teams for throwball. Knockout format.

Chess

Rapid chess rounds, Swiss pairing, time control 15+10. Open and youth categories.

Code Sprint Tech

2-hour coding challenge with beginner and advanced tracks (algorithms & practical tasks). Solo entries allowed.

Robotics Demo & Mini-Battle Tech

Bring a small robot or use provided kits. Obstacle course and friendly bot battles. Pre-registration recommended.

Tech Quiz Tech

Team quiz (3 members) covering general tech, logic puzzles, and quick-fire rounds. Great for school teams.
